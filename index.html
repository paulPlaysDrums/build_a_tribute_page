Al Bundy

Touchdown Bundy

  • Al Bundy is a simple, working-class man.
  • He was a star running back on the Polk High School football team.
  • Al Bundy's biggest accomplishment in life was scoring four touchdowns in a single game for the Polk High School Panthers in the Chicago city championship game.
  • Al is married to Peggy. They have two children: Kelly and Bud.
  • Al lives in a suburb of Chicago and is the proud owner of a 1970s Dodge automobile.
  • He works as a shoe salesman at Gary's Shoes and Accessories for Today's Woman at New Market Mall. Al hates his job and makes minimum-wage.
  • Aside from his bad luck, Al also maintains a "do-it-yourself" attitude whenever something in the house needs repair.
  • Bundy has a sarcastic and cynical sense of humor; he also has a definite love for his family.
  • He is a fan of oldies music, and a fan of westerns. His favorite movie is Hondo.
